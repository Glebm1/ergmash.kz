Modern Technologies

Our plant is equipped with cutting-edge machinery, including CNC machines, laser and plasma cutting systems, as well as specialized press and milling machines. This enables us to manufacture complex structures with high precision that meet international quality standards.

The use of modern metal processing technologies ensures high productivity and minimizes material losses. Automated welding and bending methods are applied to achieve high connection precision and structural reliability.

Automated Processes

The implementation of automated production management systems significantly reduces order fulfillment times and minimizes human error. Modern digital control systems ensure stability and predictability at every stage of production.

Automation also allows for flexible adjustments to manufacturing processes to meet specific customer requirements. This is particularly important when producing custom equipment and complex metal structures.

High-Quality Welding

Welding production is a key area of our plant's operations. We use argon arc, laser, and robotic welding methods, allowing us to create durable and load-resistant metal structures. Our welding complexes ensure maximum weld precision, which is crucial for manufacturing cranes, bridges, and heavy industrial equipment.

Every welded unit undergoes rigorous testing for airtightness and strength, eliminating defects and enhancing the reliability of the final product.

Full Cycle Metal Processing

Our plant performs a full metal processing cycle, from cutting and stamping to powder coating and final assembly. The production process actively utilizes galvanic coatings, thermal treatment, and anti-corrosion technologies, which extend the lifespan of metal structures even under the most demanding operating conditions.

Milling and turning operations are performed on high-precision computer-controlled machines, achieving processing accuracy within hundredths of a millimeter.

Multi-Stage Quality Control

Each product at our plant undergoes multiple stages of quality control. We test products for strength, vibration resistance, corrosion resistance, and compliance with technical standards. This ensures a long service life and reliable operation in various industries.

To enhance inspection accuracy, we use X-ray defectoscopes, ultrasonic diagnostics, and laboratory metal testing. We strive to meet the strictest international quality standards, including ISO 9001.

Efficient Logistics

A well-developed logistics network allows us to efficiently deliver products both domestically and internationally. The plant has its own storage facilities where finished products and materials are carefully preserved.

Modern inventory management and warehouse control systems help minimize logistics costs and ensure timely order shipments. We collaborate with leading transport companies to guarantee seamless deliveries to any region.

Continuous Development and Modernization

We are committed to continuous improvement and consistently invest in upgrading our production capabilities. The modernization of machinery, the introduction of new technologies, and employee skill development help us remain competitive in the market and offer our customers cutting-edge solutions.

In the future, we plan to implement additive manufacturing technologies (3D metal printing), which will significantly expand the plant’s capabilities and improve the precision of complex component manufacturing.
