Factory History

The Pavlodar Machine-Building Plant (PMZ) was founded in 1955 and, over its many years of history, has become one of the largest industrial enterprises in Kazakhstan. The plant specializes in the production of metal structures, lifting equipment, and industrial machinery, which are in demand across various industries.

Since its establishment, PMZ has undergone significant development, modernizing its production facilities, implementing advanced technologies, and becoming part of LLP "ERG Service". This has provided access to leading international production standards and quality management systems.

Our Technologies

The plant has implemented modern technological processes that improve work efficiency, production precision, and automation levels. We utilize digital technologies, automated systems, and innovative approaches to metal processing.

CNC Machines

Modern Computer Numerical Control (CNC) machines enable precision metal processing with minimal errors. This guarantees high accuracy of parts and increases the productivity of the enterprise.

Laser and Plasma Cutting

Laser and plasma cutting provide fast and precise metal cutting, allowing for the creation of complex structures with a high degree of accuracy. These technologies significantly reduce production waste and improve the quality of the final products.

Robotic Welding Systems

Automated welding systems ensure reliable and durable welded joints, which is especially important for lifting equipment and metal structures designed for heavy loads.

Automated Assembly Lines

The implementation of automated assembly lines allows the plant to standardize processes and significantly reduce production time without compromising quality.

Quality Control

Each product manufactured by the plant undergoes multi-stage quality control. Methods such as ultrasonic diagnostics, strength testing, and compliance verification with international standards are used to guarantee product reliability.

Energy-Saving Technologies

PMZ actively implements energy-saving technologies, reducing production costs and minimizing the environmental impact. The use of innovative solutions enhances the efficiency of the enterprise.

Production Capabilities

Our plant performs a full production cycle, from design to the release of finished products. All work stages are carried out using modern technologies and equipment.

  1. Product design and development using digital technologies
  2. Metal processing, including turning, milling, and drilling operations
  3. Assembly and welding of metal structures using robotic systems
  4. Anti-corrosion treatment and product painting
  5. Quality control at every stage of production
  6. Logistics and delivery of finished products to customers

Main Production Areas

PMZ manufactures products for various sectors of the economy, providing enterprises in Kazakhstan and CIS countries with reliable engineering solutions.

  • Construction: Production of metal structures for residential and industrial facilities
  • Industry: Lifting equipment, including overhead and gantry cranes
  • Energy Sector: Manufacturing of reservoirs, pipelines, and heat exchangers
  • Logistics: Production of conveyors and automated systems for warehouses and factories

Mission and Prospects

The Pavlodar Machine-Building Plant is focused on the development of digital manufacturing and the implementation of Industry 4.0 technologies. We aim to automate key processes, enhance energy efficiency, and develop innovative solutions for the industry.

In the future, the plant plans to expand export supplies, implement additive manufacturing technologies (3D metal printing), increase the level of production digitalization, and improve quality control systems.
