Our Capabilities

Advanced technologies, innovative solutions, and reliable production.

Crane Manufacturing

We produce overhead and gantry cranes with a lifting capacity of 5 to 140 tons. Our cranes meet international safety standards and offer high load resistance. They are used in industrial plants, warehouses, logistics complexes, and construction sites.

Grapple Manufacturing

Our factory produces various types of grapples — mechanical, hydraulic, and electromagnetic. They are widely used in the mining, metallurgical, and construction industries for loading and transporting bulk materials, scrap metal, timber, and other cargo.

Metal Structure Manufacturing

We manufacture load-bearing and auxiliary metal structures for construction, energy, mechanical engineering, and the oil and gas industry. We use modern welding, anti-corrosion treatment, and testing technologies to ensure product durability and reliability.

About the Plant

Pavlodar Machine-Building Plant (PMZ) is one of the leading manufacturers of metal structures and lifting equipment in Kazakhstan.

Founded in 1955, the plant is part of LLP "ERG Service," a centralized service enterprise of the Eurasian Group (ERG).

The plant is equipped with modern machinery, including CNC machines, laser and plasma cutting units, and automated assembly lines.
